Sasha's Vodka Pasta

Description

A wonderful winter dish, designed to warm your heart in the middle of snow.

Always use the cheapest vodka that you can find.

Ingredients

  • Vodka
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Penne
  • Cream
  • White Onion

Steps

  1. Cut and fry the onions
  2. Add vodka and fry the onions more
  3. Add the tomatoes and cover the pan
  4. Wait for the tomatoes to pop by themselves
  5. Boil the pasta
  6. Add cream and pasta to the sauce

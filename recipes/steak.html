Sasha's Steak

Description

Makes one feel like a carnivourous caveman, and restores one's inner strength.

If you cook your steak more than medium rare than we are not friends.

Ingredients

  • Steak

Steps

  1. Season one side of the steak right before frying
  2. Fry one side of the steak
  3. Season the second side of the steak and flip
  4. Sit back and enjoy
